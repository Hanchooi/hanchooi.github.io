US Demography in 1994: A Power BI Report

An attempt to visualize the demographics of the United States in 1994, employing Power BI tools to provide insights and an overview.

Key Findings
  • Gender disparity in occupations, with certain professions being predominantly male or female.
  • Earnings disparities based on factors such as education level, native country, and industry.
  • Differences in marital status and its correlation with demographic characteristics.
  • Insights into the age distribution across various demographic subgroups.

Analysis of Australia's National Enrollment using R Studio

An attempt to analyze and visualize National Enrollment Data from the year 2005 to 2017 in order to gain insights into:

  • Popularity of universities and their relation to G8 status and regions.
  • Field of study that each university and region specializes in (if any).
  • Differences in the preferences of International Students and Domestic Students (region and field of study).
  • Indication of gender-segregation in the field of study.
  • Yearly trends of the number of students enrolled in higher institutions.
  • ATAR distributions in different types of institutions.
